Meet Dr. Sayli A. Kharche

Dr. Sayli A. Kharche is a licensed Clinical Psychologist based in Pune, with a passion for helping individuals, couples, and families lead healthier, more fulfilling lives.

With a warm, non-judgmental approach rooted in evidence-based practices, she creates a safe space where healing begins. Her expertise spans cognitive behavioral therapy, mindfulness-based interventions, and psychological assessments.

She believes that seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness.

M.Phil Clinical Psychology RCI Licensed 5+ Years Experience

How Can I Help You?

Every journey is unique. Explore the services tailored to support your well-being.

Individual Therapy

Personalized one-on-one sessions to help you work through anxiety, depression, stress, trauma, and other personal challenges at your own pace.

Child Psychology

Specialized support for children dealing with behavioral issues, learning difficulties, emotional challenges, and developmental concerns in a safe, playful environment.

Couple Counseling

Strengthen your relationship through guided conversations that improve communication, rebuild trust, and help you navigate conflicts together.

Corporate Wellness

Workshops, stress management programs, and employee wellness initiatives designed to foster a healthier, more productive workplace culture.

Online Sessions

Psychological Assessments

Comprehensive psychological testing for IQ, personality, career aptitude, learning disabilities, and clinical diagnosis using standardized tools.
